Our goal


In the absence of a generic system to manage sensors allowing the coexistence of devices from distinct types and contexts, we’ve created BioMonit.

BioMonit is an app that allows monitoring any type of sensor in real time and visualizing the history of previous measures. Also, you can export the info and measures from the sensors in several formats.

Our main goal is to manage several types of sensors simultaneously, without hindrance, allowing any company, hospital or family to manage thy sensors without any problem or difficulty.
